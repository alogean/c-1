## General Strategies for Legal Access

1. **University Library Access:** Most academic institutions provide access to scientific journals
2. **Author Contact:** Researchers often share their work when contacted directly
3. **ResearchGate/Academia.edu:** Check if authors have uploaded legal copies
4. **Preprint Servers:** Look for preprint versions (arXiv, bioRxiv, etc.)
5. **Open Access Button:** Use https://openaccessbutton.org/ to find legal versions
6. **Unpaywall Browser Extension:** Automatically finds legal open access versions

## Recommended Actions

1. Start with the confirmed open access article (#7)
2. Check your institutional access for subscription-based journals
3. Contact authors directly for articles not freely available
4. Use the provided DOIs to access articles through official channels

## Note on Copyright

Always respect copyright laws and access articles only through legal means. The links provided above direct to official publisher pages or confirmed open access repositories.
